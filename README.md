## Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing a hypothetical organic cosmetics startup using lean startup and business analytics principles. The goal was to evaluate market potential, customer segments, and key business metrics to support data-driven decision-making.

- Analyze customer segments and value propositions
- Identify key business metrics (KPIs)

## Key Analysis Areas
- Market and customer analysis
- Value proposition and revenue streams
- Business Model Canvas
- KPI identification and performance evaluation

## Tools & Concepts Used
- Business Analytics
- Lean Startup Methodology
- KPI Analysis
- Data-driven decision-making

## Outcome
The analysis provided clear insights into customer needs, pricing strategies, and growth opportunities for the organic cosmetics business.
